# Life Rhythm (ADHD-friendly)

## Core principle
Energy and focus come in waves.
The system must work with this, not against it.

---

## Daily rhythm

### Morning (activation)
Goal: wake up brain, not be productive.

Rules:
- No decisions.
- No planning.
- Light movement or simple routine only.

---

### Main work block (focus window)
Goal: ONE finished thing.

Rules:
- Only one active task.
- Use Focus & Execution Protocol.
- New ideas go to Idea Inbox.
- Stop after the finish line.

---

### Secondary block (low pressure)
Goal: maintenance, not creation.

Allowed:
- Messages
- Payments
- Admin
- Light thinking

---

### Evening (decompression)
Goal: reduce mental noise.

Rules:
- No starting anything new.
- No “just quickly”.
- Let ideas accumulate, not execute.

---

## Weekly rhythm

### Once per week
- Weekly Money Check
- Review Payment Radar
- Clear Idea Inbox (choose 0–1 items to activate)
