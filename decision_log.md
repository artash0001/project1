# Decision Log

## Purpose
Store decisions so they do not have to be re-made.

A decision written here is considered DONE.

---

## Decision Template

### Decision
(Short description)

### Context
Why this decision was needed.

### Options considered
- Option A
- Option B
- Option C (if any)

### Chosen option
(What was decided)

### Reasoning
Why this option was chosen.

### Risks accepted
What might go wrong and is accepted.

### Date
(YYYY-MM-DD)

---

## Assistant rule
If a similar question appears again,
refer back to this decision before reconsidering.
