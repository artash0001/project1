# Payment Radar

## Rule
This file exists to prevent surprise pressure.
No amounts. No accounting. Only risk awareness.

---

## High-risk obligations (can cause serious damage)
- Car loan (risk of confiscation)
- Apartment rent
- Utilities
- Child-related critical expenses (e.g. surgery)

---

## Medium-risk obligations (stress, penalties, noise)
- Internet
- Mobile
- Other recurring services

---

## Chaos risks
- Payments that are easy to forget.
- Obligations without clear reminders.
- Things I avoid looking at.
