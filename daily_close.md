## When
At the end of the day.
2 minutes max.

## Questions
1. What ONE thing did I finish today?
2. Was today a good day? (Yes / No)
3. What MUST wait until tomorrow?

## Rules
- No fixing.
- No planning.
- Just acknowledgment.
