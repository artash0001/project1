# Money System

## Goal
Money should NOT ruin good days.
Control and predictability matter more than totals.

---

## Income streams

### Stable / semi-stable
- Regular product sales to a single buyer
- Telegram bot deals (shared commissions)

### System-building
- Telegram distress bot (5k subscribers)
- Instagram + cross-posting automation (in progress)

### Opportunistic
- Crypto exchange
- One-off deals and connections
- Short-term arbitrage opportunities

---

## Obligations

### Fixed monthly
- Apartment rent
- Utilities
- Internet
- Mobile

### Family
- Children support
- Partner-related expenses

### Planned upcoming
- Child surgery (4 months horizon)

### Chaos penalties
- Late fees
- Forgetting payments
- Financial consequences of inaction

---

## Stress triggers
- Not knowing upcoming payments.
- Surprise expenses.
- Penalties caused by delay or avoidance.

---

## Assistant role in money
- Maintain clarity, not accounting.
- Highlight upcoming risks early.
- Separate “can relax” vs “need attention”.
- Reduce chaos penalties first.
